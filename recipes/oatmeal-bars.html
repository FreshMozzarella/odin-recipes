Oatmeal Bars

Description

These oatmeal bars are easy to make, easy to scale, and are packed with protein. You can omit the sugar if you'd like. Make sure the bananas are very brown as this will be most of the natural flavoring. Perfect for baking in bulk and great for your gut! For extra moist bars you can even add applesauce

Yield: About 12 bars

Total Time: 25 minutes

Calories: 195 kcal

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas ripened and large; mashed
  • ½ cup peanut butter or any kind of nut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ¾ cups quick oats
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder, or vanilla
  • ½ cup dark chocolate chips
  • ½ cup peanuts roasted and chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 °F and line a baking pan with parchment paper. Set aside
  2. In a bowl, mash bananas with a fork or a potato masher until soft
  3. Stir the remaining ingredients. Evenly spread and gently press the mixture into prepared baking pan
  4. Bake the bars for about 15 minutes or until lightly golden on top
  5. Allow the oat bars to cool on a wire rack before cutting into squares
