Maple Salmon

Description

This salmon is easy to make and is very healthy for you

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Additional time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 1 hour

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup real maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound salmon

Steps

  1. Stir maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl
  2. Cut salmon into 4 equal-sized fillets; place in a shallow glass baking dish and coat with maple syrup mixture. Cover the dish and marinate salmon in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, turning halfway
  3. Preheat the oven 400 °F (200 °C)
  4. Place the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake salmon uncovered until flesh easily flakes with a fork, about 20 minutes
